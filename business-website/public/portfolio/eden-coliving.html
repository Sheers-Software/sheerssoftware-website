What is the minimum stay at Eden? +

Our minimum lease term is 3 months. After the initial period, you can extend on a monthly rolling basis â€” giving you the flexibility to stay as long as you love it. We also offer 6-month and 12-month packages with discounted rates.

How much is the security deposit? +

We require a refundable security deposit of 2 months' rent, paid alongside your first month's rent at signing. Deposits are fully refunded within 14 business days of move-out, provided there are no outstanding damages or charges.

Are all units fully furnished? +

Yes â€” every Eden unit comes fully furnished to a premium standard. Think quality bed frames, mattresses, sofa, dining table, smart TV, and full kitchen setup. All you need to bring are your personal belongings. We can also accommodate requests for additional items.

What is the guest policy? +

Residents are welcome to host guests in their units. For overnight guests staying more than 7 consecutive nights, we ask that you register them with reception. Guests must adhere to our community guidelines and common area rules at all times.

Are pets allowed? +

We're a proudly pet-friendly community. Cats and dogs up to 15kg are welcome in all units. There's a small refundable pet deposit of RM 500. We have designated pet areas, a weekly pet wash station, and waste bag dispensers throughout the property.

Your monthly rent includes high-speed fibre WiFi (500Mbps), water, electricity (up to a fair use limit of RM 150/month â€” excess billed separately), building maintenance, and weekly common area cleaning. Residents in our all-inclusive packages also get weekly unit cleaning included.

Parking bays are available at an additional RM 150/month per bay. EV charging bays are RM 200/month. Bays are subject to availability â€” we recommend reserving yours when you sign your lease. Monthly reserved parking is booked via the Eden app.
