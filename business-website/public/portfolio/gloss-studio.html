03 / Process

The Process

Six methodical stages. Zero shortcuts.

01
STEP 01 //
Assessment
15 MIN

Full exterior and interior inspection. Paint thickness measurement. Quote confirmed before any work begins.

02
STEP 02 //
Wash
60 MIN

pH-neutral two-bucket hand wash. Wheel arches, brake dust removal, tyre scrub. Pressure rinse and air dry.

03
STEP 03 //
Clay Bar
45 MIN

Full-panel clay bar decontamination. Removes bonded contaminants, rail dust, and industrial fallout from paintwork.

04
STEP 04 //
Polish
VARIES

Machine polishing with dual-action or rotary depending on defect severity. Swirl, scratch, and oxidation correction.

05
STEP 05 //
Protect
VARIES

Carnauba wax, sealant, or 9H ceramic coating applied by hand or machine. Protection layer cured under controlled conditions.

06
STEP 06 //
Inspect
30 MIN

Final quality control under specialist lighting. Panel-by-panel sign-off. Documentation photographed for your records.
