Culinary Experiences

Dining at The Mosaic

Two distinct venues. One unwavering commitment to exceptional taste.

The Brass Spoon

All-Day Dining

Daily  6:00 AM – 10:00 PM

A celebration of Malaysian flavours reimagined through a modern lens. From nasi lemak served the right way to beautifully crafted afternoon sets — The Brass Spoon is where the day begins and unhurried evenings unfold.

The Rooftop Bar

Sunset Cocktails & Late Nights

Daily  5:00 PM – 1:00 AM

Perched above the city, The Rooftop Bar captures Johor Bahru’s golden-hour glow. Signature cocktails inspired by Spice Route trade, curated natural wines, and live sets on Fridays. Dress to impress.

Beyond the Room

Experiences & Facilities

Every detail curated for those who expect more.

Infinity Pool

An 18-metre horizon pool with unobstructed views of the city skyline. Open daily from 7AM to 10PM.

Mosaique Spa

Signature treatments inspired by the ancient healing traditions of the Malay archipelago. Wellness rituals from 10AM to 10PM.

Fitness Studio

State-of-the-art Technogym equipment, personal training on request, and a dedicated yoga terrace. Open 24 hours.

Business Centre

Two boardrooms, high-speed fibre, printing and AV support. Ideal for executive retreats and confidential meetings. 24-hour access.

Valet Parking

Complimentary valet service for in-house guests. Secure, covered multi-level carpark with EV charging stations.

Concierge

Your dedicated Mosaic host. Dinner reservations, private transfers, city tours, yacht charters — nothing is too much to ask.
