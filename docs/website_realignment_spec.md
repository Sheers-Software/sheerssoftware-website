# Prompt & Specification for Website Realignment: Nocturn AI by SheersSoft

## 1. Context & Business Pivot
- **Company:** Sheers Software Sdn Bhd (Malaysia).
- **Product:** Nocturn AI (Hospitality AI Concierge).
- **The Pivot:** We are moving from a "SaaS Subscription" model (RM299/mo) to a "Result-Based Revenue Partner" model.
- **The Primary Value Proposition:** Nocturn AI recovers "leaked" direct bookings on WhatsApp, Web, and Email, allowing hotels to bypass high OTA commissions (15%–25% from Agoda/Booking.com).
- **The Pricing Model:** RM199/mo Platform Fee + 3% Commission on Facilitated Bookings + RM999 One-time Setup/AI Training Fee.
- **Goal:** Position Nocturn AI as a "Zero-Risk" profit center to maximize 30-day cash collected and long-term MRR via commissions.

## 2. Target Audience & Tone
- **Primary Persona:** Malaysian Hotel General Managers (GMs), Owners, and Revenue Managers.
- **Tone:** Professional, ROI-driven, PMP-certified precision, locally grounded (Malaysian hospitality context), and authoritative.
- **Key Trigger:** The frustration of paying "digital taxes" (commissions) to OTAs for guests who are already on the hotel's own channels.

## 3. Compliance & Technical Standards (Malaysia 2026)
- **PDPA 2024/2025:** Must emphasize compliance with the latest Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act (Mandatory DPO, Breach Notification, Consent for Automated Profiling).
- **LHDN E-Invoicing:** Ready for the August 2026 mandate (Self-billing and individual e-invoice support).
- **Visit Malaysia Year 2026 (VMY2026):** Position as the essential tool for the 2026 tourism surge.

## 4. Website Architecture & Copy Requirements

### A. Hero Section (The Hook)
- **Headline:** Stop Paying 18% to OTAs. Capture Direct Revenue for 3%.
- **Sub-headline:** Nocturn AI turns your WhatsApp, Email, and Website into a high-converting sales machine. Pay only when you get a confirmed booking. Built specifically for the Malaysian hospitality market.
- **CTA 1:** Calculate Your Revenue Recovery (Primary).
- **CTA 2:** Start 48-Hour Implementation (Secondary - RM999).

### B. The "Revenue Leak" Problem
- **Define the "3 AM Gap":** When staff is asleep, guests go to Agoda to get instant answers. You lose 18% immediately.
- **Solution:** Nocturn AI bridges this gap with 2-minute response times in BM, English, and local dialects.

### C. Feature Set (Realigned as Benefits)
- **Omni-Channel Sales Agent:** Handles WhatsApp, Web, and Email inquiries under one revenue-tracking roof.
- **The Daily GM Report:** (Crucial Highlight) An automated 9:00 AM email summarizing revenue recovered, OTA fees saved, and guest sentiment.
- **Local Hospitality Logic:** Pre-trained on Malaysian hotel operations (SST, Tourism Tax, local check-in norms).
- **Human-in-the-Loop:** Seamless handoff for VIP inquiries or complex requests, ensuring no lead is ever lost.
