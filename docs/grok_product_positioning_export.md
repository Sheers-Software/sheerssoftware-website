### Key Insights from the Conversation:
*   **Target Audience:** Independent 3-4 star Malaysian hotels (40-150 rooms) that are heavily reliant on WhatsApp/email for bookings and face significant "OTA bleed."
*   **Core Problem:** High OTA commissions (15-30%), delayed payouts (30-60 days), and lost direct bookings due to slow manual responses after hours.
*   **Solution Positioning:** Nocturn AI is an "always-on AI Revenue Recovery Layer" that captures and converts manual inquiries into direct bookings using 24/7 local-language automation (BM/English).
*   **Value Proposition:** 15%+ recovered direct revenue, same-day cash flow via FPX/DuitNow integration, and 15-30% savings on OTA commissions.
*   **Strategic Approach:** A "Hybrid Co-Pilot" mode is recommended to bypass current WhatsApp Business API verification hurdles (due to home-office address issues), allowing for immediate value delivery without high capital investment or complex PMS integrations.
